FEMAX

HCI Bootcamp design workshop / Family gathering / ToC app

10/2018

For us international students, we are far away from home and miss our family and friends back there. We have been contacting them with all kinds of social media apps. We feel close to them when we see them online, and we don't need their exact location to feel them around: the green little dots representing their presence make us reassured.

The same feeling of reassurance is needed by Anna's family. She is a 32-year-old housewife who has a daughter. She wants to make sure her family members are safe and sound, especially when they live far away. So we decided to design for her.
